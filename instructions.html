BASIC GAMEPLAY:

  • Fill in your names in text boxes given below and click on the "Submit" button.
  • Click on the Canvas.
  • You need to click on the "Yes!" or "Nope!" first.
  • Remember you have to click on the "Yes!" or "Nope!" each and every time you play..
  • By clicking the "Yes" button you confirm that you are prepared to buy the place you land on in the coming six places. If the money you have is not enough you lose the game. SO BEWARE!!
  • By clicking the "No" button you confirm that you will not buy the place you land on in the coming six places but you will not be able to earn money either. So that means there is a little bit of luck involved in the game as well ;)
  • Your given pegs will move according to the number you get on the dice.
  • Have Fun!!!!.

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. You get Rs200/- on each start.
  2. On going to jail you lose Rs200/-.
  3. On landing on the +100, you get Rs100
  4. On going to parking area Rs.50/-will be deducted.
  5. If you are lucky enough you will get Rs.400/- on landing on treasure..!!
  6. When you land on a trap, you go back to the start.There are two traps in the game and remember you couldn't get any additional money on going to start..
  7. If you land on Shoppers Express you get Rs125/- cashback...
